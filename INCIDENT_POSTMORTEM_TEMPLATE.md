# Incident Postmortem Template

Use this file after outages, exploit incidents, broken releases, severe regressions, or operational failures.

## Postmortem law

- Do not stop at symptom description.
- Do not hide timeline gaps.
- Do not call it resolved without follow-up owners.

## Required sections

- incident title
- severity
- start time
- detection time
- mitigation time
- recovery time
- player impact
- business impact
- root cause
- contributing causes
- why safeguards failed
- action items
- action owners
